Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to the questions we hear most

How long does it take to build a website?

Timeline depends on the package. Basic Package takes about 1 week, Standard Package takes 2 weeks, and Premium Package timeline will be confirmed during consultation. We'll give you a specific date when we start.

What information do you need from me to get started?

We'll need your business information, brand assets (logo, brand colors if you have them), the content for your website (text and images), and any specific preferences or examples of sites you like.

Do you offer website hosting?

Yes — we can recommend and set up hosting for you. We work with reliable providers to ensure your site is fast, secure, and always online.

Can I update the website myself after it's built?

Yes! We build with user-friendly systems so you can make updates yourself. We also provide training after launch so you feel confident managing your content.

What is your payment policy?

We require a 50% deposit before starting work, with the remaining 50% due upon completion before the website goes live. You'll see a full preview for approval first.
