Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services

How long does it take to build a website?

The timeline depends on the complexity of your project. Our Basic Package takes about 1 week, Standard Package takes 2 weeks, and Premium Package takes 3 weeks. We'll provide a specific timeline during our initial consultation.

What information do you need from me to get started?

We'll need your business information, brand assets (logo, colors, etc.), content for your website (text and images), and any specific requirements or preferences you have for your website.

Do you offer website hosting?

Yes, we can recommend and set up hosting for your website. We work with reliable hosting providers to ensure your website is fast, secure, and always available.

Can I update the website myself after it's built?

Yes, we build websites with user-friendly content management systems that allow you to make updates yourself. We also provide training on how to update your content.

What is your payment policy?

We require a 50% deposit before starting work, with the remaining 50% due upon completion of the project before the website goes live.
