FAQ Section
FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services

How long does it take to build a website?

The timeline depends on the complexity of your project. A basic website typically takes 2-3 weeks, while more complex sites with custom features can take 6-8 weeks. We'll provide a detailed timeline during our initial consultation.

Do I need to provide content for my website?

While we can help with content creation, having your own content ready speeds up the process. We recommend providing text, images, and any specific information you want on your site. If needed, we can also assist with professional copywriting services.

Will my website be mobile-friendly?

All our websites are built with a mobile-first approach, ensuring they look great and function perfectly on all devices - smartphones, tablets, and desktops. We test on multiple devices before launch.

Can I update my website myself?

Yes! We can build your website with an easy-to-use content management system that allows you to make updates without technical knowledge. We also provide training and documentation to help you manage your site confidently.

What if I need changes after my website is live?

We offer ongoing maintenance and support services. Whether you need minor updates or major changes, we're here to help. You can choose from our maintenance packages or request changes on an as-needed basis.

Do you offer hosting services?

While we don't provide hosting directly, we can recommend reliable hosting providers and help you set up your hosting account. We can also manage the hosting for you as part of our maintenance services.

What is your payment structure?

We typically require a 50% deposit to start your project, with the remaining 50% due upon completion. For larger projects, we can arrange a payment plan. We accept bank transfers, EFT, and other payment methods.

Will my website be optimized for search engines?

Yes! We implement basic SEO best practices in all our websites, including proper meta tags, heading structure, and fast loading speeds. For more advanced SEO strategies, we offer dedicated SEO optimization services.
